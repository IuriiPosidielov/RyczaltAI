Polish Tax Law — Tax Rates (2023)

1) The tax rate specified in Art. 41(1) and (13), Art. 109(2), Art. 110, and Art. 138i(4) is 23%.  
2) The tax rate specified in Art. 41(2), Art. 120(2) and (3), and Title of Annex No. 3 is 8%.  
3) The rate of refundable tax credit under Art. 115(2) is 7%.  
4) The flat-rate tax under Art. 114(1) is 4%.
