Polish Tax Law – Article 146d: Minister of Finance’s Authority to Adjust VAT Rates

The Minister of Finance, by means of regulations, may:

1. During the period specified in Article 146aa, reduce VAT rates to 0%, 5%, or 8% for the supply of certain goods and services, or for parts of such supplies, and define the conditions for applying reduced rates.

2. For a limited period not exceeding January 31, 2011, reduce the 8% VAT rate to 7% for the supply of certain goods and services, or for parts thereof, and define the conditions for applying the 7% rate.

When issuing such regulations, the Minister must consider:

1. The specific nature of trade in certain goods and services;

2. The state budget execution;

3. EU regulations.

VAT rate adjustments under Polish tax law, governed by Article 146d, allow temporary reductions to 0%, 5%, or 7% for specific goods/services, subject to ministerial regulation and EU compliance.
