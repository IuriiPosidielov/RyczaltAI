**Customs Exemptions – Import Tax Relief for Funeral Goods**

Art. 79. Exemption from import duty applies to:

1) Caskets containing deceased persons’ remains, urns with ashes, and flowers, wreaths, and other decorative items typically accompanying funerals;

2) Flowers, wreaths, and other decorative items brought by individuals residing in a third country, traveling to attend funerals or to decorate graves located within the country’s territory, provided the quantity does not indicate commercial intent.

— Exemption applies to funeral-related items and personal tribute goods imported for memorial purposes, excluding commercial quantities.
