The provisions of the Act do not apply to:  
1) transactions involving the sale of a business or an organized part of a business;  
2) acts that cannot be the subject of a legally effective contract.

This article excludes application of the Act to business sales and acts that are inherently uncontractable under law.
Exclusion applies to business transfers and inherently uncontractable acts under applicable law.
