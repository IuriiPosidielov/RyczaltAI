Employment Law – Polish Labor Code – Validity of Financial Ministry Certificates

A certificate referred to in Article 21b(7)(6) of the Act cited in Article 175, issued by the Minister of Finance prior to May 1, 2004, remains valid until a new certificate is issued or until the expiration date of its validity period.

This provision governs the legal continuity of financial ministry-issued employment certificates under Polish labor law before May 1, 2004.
