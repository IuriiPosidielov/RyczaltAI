EU VAT Law – Intracommunity Delivery of New Transport Vehicles – Filing Requirements

In the case of an intracommunity delivery of new transport vehicles to a buyer referred to in Article 13(2)(4), the taxpayer shall, within 14 days of delivery, transmit a copy of the issued invoice or the data contained therein to the designated entity specified in Article 97(17).

The above requirement does not apply to structured invoices or invoices referred to in Article 106nda(1), Article 106nf(1), and Article 106nh(1), which have been transmitted to the National e-Invoice System prior to the expiry of the 14-day period from the date of intracommunity delivery of new transport vehicles.
