Customs Law – Article 38: Application of Customs Duties and Procedures for Debt Assessment

In areas not covered by Art. 17 para. 1 pts. 1–2 and para. 1b, Art. 19a paras. 9–11, and Art. 33–37, customs duty provisions governing collection and amount shall apply, excluding provisions on payment deferral, installment arrangements, and other payment facilitations under customs law.

If, pursuant to customs law, notification of the customs debt amount cannot be issued due to statute of limitations, but less than five years have elapsed since the end of the calendar year in which the import-related tax obligation arose, and there is a basis for calculating or verifying the tax liability, the head of the customs and tax office may determine calculation elements in accordance with customs law provisions to properly assess the customs duty due on imported goods.
