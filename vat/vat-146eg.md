Tax Calculation for VAT-Exempt Services in Trade and Catering – Poland

In the case of services provided by the taxpayer, including those in trade and catering, during the period referred to in Article 146ef, the tax amount due under Article 85 is calculated as the product of the delivery value and the following rates:  
1) 18.70% – for goods and services subject to the 23% VAT rate;  
2) 7.41% – for goods and services subject to the 8% VAT rate.
