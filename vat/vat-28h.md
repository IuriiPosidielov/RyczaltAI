Service Provision Location for Non-Taxable Service Recipients:  
In cases where services are provided to entities that are not taxable service recipients — specifically, auxiliary transport services (e.g., loading, unloading, transloading, or similar) or valuation of movable tangible assets — the place of service provision is determined by the actual location where the services are performed.
