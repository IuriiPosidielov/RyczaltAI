The location of services related to real estate, including those provided by appraisers, real estate brokers, hotel accommodations or similar facilities (e.g., holiday resorts or camping sites), use and enjoyment of real estate, and services for planning and coordinating construction work (e.g., architects and construction supervision), shall be determined by the location of the real property involved.

Real estate-related services, brokerage, accommodation, construction coordination, and property usage are governed by the geographic location of the property under Polish civil law.
