Under Article 18, enforcement authorities designated by the Act of June 17, 1966, on Enforcement Proceedings in Administration (Journal of Laws 2025, items 132 and 620), as well as bailiffs, performing enforcement actions under the Civil Procedure Code, are liable for VAT on deliveries of goods belonging to the debtor or held by them in violation of applicable laws, carried out in the course of enforcement.

This provision addresses VAT liability for enforcement agents executing civil enforcement actions, specifically relating to the delivery of debtor-owned goods under Polish civil enforcement law.
