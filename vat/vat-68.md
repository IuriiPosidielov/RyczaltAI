Tax Exemption for Import of Advertising Printed Materials — Poland

Art. 68 provides for exemption from import VAT on printed advertising materials—specifically catalogs, price lists, user manuals, or promotional brochures—intended for sale, rental, or use in transport, trade, banking, or insurance services, imported by a person established in a third country, subject to the following conditions:

1. The name of the enterprise producing, selling, or renting the goods or offering the related services must be visibly displayed on the materials.

2. The shipment must contain one document or one copy of each document, if multiple documents are included.

3. For shipments containing multiple copies of the same document, the total gross weight must not exceed 1 kg.

4. The shipment must not be part of a series of shipments sent by the same sender to the same recipient.
