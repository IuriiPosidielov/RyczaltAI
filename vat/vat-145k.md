Tax Law – Administrative Offense Regulation – Article 145k

1. Any person who, contrary to the obligation, fails to submit the notification referred to in Article 145i, paragraph 1, or submits it after the deadline or provides inaccurate information inconsistent with actual circumstances, shall be subject to a fine for a tax offense.

2. The same penalty applies to anyone who, contrary to the obligation, fails to submit a notification of changes to data contained in the notification referred to in Article 145i, paragraph 1, or submits it after the deadline or provides inaccurate information inconsistent with actual circumstances.
