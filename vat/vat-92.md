The Minister of Finance, by means of regulation, may define:

1. Cases in which the amount of tax charged may be refunded to the entity acquiring goods or services (import), as well as the conditions and procedure for such refunds.

2. When issuing the regulations under paragraph 1, the Minister must consider:

   - The specific nature of certain business activities and economic conditions affecting specific goods;
   - The necessity of proper documentation for transactions entitling to tax refunds;
   - The need to ensure equal treatment of entities using the same goods in their operations;
   - EU law provisions.
