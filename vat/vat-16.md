Article 16. Taxpayers also include legal entities, organizational units without legal personality, and natural persons who are not taxpayers as referred to in Article 15, performing occasional intra-community deliveries of new transport vehicles.

VAT-registered entities and individuals occasionally delivering new transport vehicles within the EU may be classified as taxpayers under this provision.
