Customs Duty Exemption for Personal Imports — Poland

This article establishes customs duty exemptions for personal imports delivered from third-country territories by individuals to other individuals residing within the country, subject to specific conditions and quantitative limits.

Conditions for Exemption (Art. 52.1):
- Goods must not indicate commercial intent;
- Recipient is not obligated to pay sender-related fees;
- The shipment must be occasional in nature.

Exempt Quantities (Art. 52.2):
- Alcoholic beverages: 1L for spirits >22% ABV or 80%+; 1L for spirits ≤22%, wines, etc.;
- Tobacco: 50 cigarettes, 25 cigars (≤3g), 10 cigars, or 50g tobacco;
- Perfumes: 50g; toilet water: 0.25L;
- Coffee: 500g; coffee extracts: 200g;
- Tea: 100g; tea extracts: 40g.

Value Limit (Art. 52.3):
- Total shipment value must not exceed 45 EUR equivalent in PLN.

Excess Quantities (Art. 52.4):
- Goods exceeding specified limits are fully excluded from exemption.
