Tax Law – Polish Tax Code – Article 109a: Liability for Tax Invoices Without Tax Identification Number

In the case of a taxpayer referred to in Article 109(3), who has recorded an issued invoice concerning a sale confirmed by a receipt lacking the tax identification number, the tax authority shall impose an additional tax liability equal to 100% of the tax amount indicated on the invoice. This provision does not apply to natural persons liable for administrative or criminal tax offenses for the same act.
