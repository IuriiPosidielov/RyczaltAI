Legal Text: Polish VAT e-Invoicing System – Eligible Users and Structured Invoicing Requirements

The following entities may use the National e-Invoicing System (Krajowy System e-Faktur):

1. Taxpayers;
2. Entities designated by taxpayers;
3. Entities referred to in Article 106c;
4. Natural persons designated by entities referred to in Article 106c;
5. Natural persons indicated in notifications concerning the granting or revocation of rights to use the National e-Invoicing System, to whom taxpayers or entities referred to in Article 106c have granted such rights — this provision (point 5) will take effect from February 1, 2026 (Journal of Laws 2023, item 1598; 2024, item 852 and 1721);
6. Other entities not listed in points 1–5, designated by natural persons using the system, provided such designation is permitted under rights granted to those natural persons — subject to authentication as specified in regulations issued under Article 106r(3).

Article 106nc:

1. Structured invoices must be issued and received via the National e-Invoicing System using electronic interface software, in electronic form, and in accordance with the electronic document format defined by law (Act of February 17, 2005, on the informatization of public sector entities). This provision will take effect from February 1, 2026 (Journal of Laws 2023, item 1598; 2024, items 852 and 1721).

2. The electronic interface software referred to in paragraph 1 is available on the website address published in the Public Information Bulletin on the official website of the ministry responsible for public finance.

3. The Minister responsible for public finance provides, via the electronic platform of public administration services, the official template for structured invoices.
