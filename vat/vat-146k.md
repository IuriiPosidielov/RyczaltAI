This provision establishes the minimum percentage thresholds for qualifying electronic payment transactions to meet eligibility requirements for VAT refund under Article 87(6d) of the Polish VAT Act, applicable in two distinct periods:  
- From January 1, 2022, to June 30, 2023: Minimum 65% of total sales (including VAT) recorded via cash registers connected to the Central Cash Register Repository, where payments via card, mobile payment, or payment instruction services (as defined in the Payment Services Act of August 19, 2011) must constitute at least 65% of total sales.  
- From July 1, 2023, to June 30, 2025:  
  - For Article 87(6e)(1)(a): Minimum 70% of total sales (including VAT) recorded via cash registers meeting specific technical requirements (Article 87(6e)(4)(c)) must constitute at least 70% of total sales.  
  - For Article 87(6e)(1)(b): Minimum 55% of total sales (including VAT) recorded via cash registers connected to the Central Cash Register Repository, with payments via card, mobile payment, or payment instruction services, must constitute at least 55% of total sales.  

The total sales value is determined based on data from the Central Cash Register Repository, excluding sales documented via invoices under Article 106h(2). For Article 1a(1), total sales value is based on records under Article 109(3).
