Tax Law – Polish Fiscal Policy – Tax Rate Adjustment Authority

The Minister of Finance, by means of an ordinance in 2023, may reduce tax rates to 0%, 5%, or 8% for the supply of certain goods and services, or for portions thereof, and may establish the conditions under which such reduced rates apply. When issuing such an ordinance, the Minister shall take into account: (1) the specific nature of trade in certain goods and services; (2) the progress of state budget execution.

Summary: Tax rate reduction authority under Polish fiscal law, applicable in 2023, subject to budgetary and market-specific considerations.
