VAT Import Procedure Agent Obligations – Polish Tax Law

An agent appointed under Article 138d performs on behalf of and for the taxpayer for whom they are designated all obligations related to: filing an intent declaration for import VAT procedure, VAT settlement in import procedures (including preparation of VAT declarations and payment), maintaining and storing documentation and records, providing access to such documentation, and other duties arising under this chapter. The agent is jointly liable with the taxpayer for any tax obligations settled on the taxpayer’s behalf.

VAT import agent responsibilities under Polish tax law include filing intent declarations, VAT settlement, documentation management, and joint liability for tax obligations.
