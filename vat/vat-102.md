Employment Law / Public Finance Regulation — Article 102

The competent minister for public finance shall, by regulation, establish the format of the summary report together with instructions regarding its completion, submission deadlines, and submission location; also establish the format of the correction to the summary report, including instructions for its completion, taking into account the specific nature of certain activities and EU regulations.
