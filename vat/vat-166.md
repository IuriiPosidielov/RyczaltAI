Tax Refund Provisions for Goods and Services Purchased or Imported in 2003 or January 1–April 30, 2004, by Entities Defined in Art. 89(1)(3)

The provisions governing tax refunds for goods and services acquired or imported during the specified period apply those issued under Art. 23(1)(4) of the law referenced in Art. 175.

This provision addresses VAT refund eligibility for specific entities and timeframes, governed by secondary legislation derived from statutory authority. Relevant to Polish VAT law and import transaction compliance.

Tax refund rules for 2003–early 2004 apply to entities defined in Art. 89(1)(3), governed by regulations issued under Art. 23(1)(4) of the referenced law.
