Tax Exemption for Import of Goods for War Memorial Construction and Maintenance

Art. 78. Exemption from import tax applies to goods imported from third-country territory by authorized organizations, for use in the construction, maintenance, or decoration of cemeteries, graves, and war memorial monuments located on the territory of the country, commemorating war victims.
