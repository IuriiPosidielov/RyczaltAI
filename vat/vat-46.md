**Customs Law – Definitions for Articles 47–80**

For the purposes of Articles 47–80:

1) “Alcoholic beverages” include beer, wine, wine-based or alcohol-based aperitifs, cognacs, liqueurs, spirituous drinks, and similar products classified under Harmonized System (HS) codes 2203 00 to 2208.

2) “Tobacco and tobacco products” include goods classified under HS codes 2401 to 2403, and 2404 11 00 and 2404 19 10.

3) “Personal-use goods” are items directly used by individuals or used in household settings, where quantity or type does not indicate commercial or business intent, and portable equipment necessary for a person’s professional activity.
