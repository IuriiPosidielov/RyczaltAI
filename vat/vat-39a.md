Tax Liability – Third-Party Responsibility – Jurisdictional Authority

The tax authority competent to decide on matters of third-party liability for taxpayer’s tax arrears relating to import duties is the authority referred to in Article 66(1a) of the Act of March 19, 2004 – Customs Law.
