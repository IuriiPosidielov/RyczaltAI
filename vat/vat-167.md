Employment Tax Law – Article 167: Minimum Threshold for Tax Refund Eligibility

The minimum total value of purchases including VAT, referred to in Article 21e(2)(2) of the law specified in Article 175, as derived from documents issued under Article 21c(2) of that same law, applies only during the period in which those documents served as the legal basis for refunding VAT to travelers, under the provisions of the law.

— Tax refund eligibility based on travel-related VAT documents under Polish VAT law.
