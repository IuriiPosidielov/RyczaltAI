Tax Calculation for Services in Trade and Catering – Poland

In the case of services provided by the taxpayer, including within the scope of trade and catering, during the period referred to in Article 146aa, the tax amount due for purposes of Article 85 is calculated as the product of the delivery value and the following rates:  
1) 18.70% – for goods and services subject to the 23% tax rate;  
2) 7.41% – for goods and services subject to the 8% tax rate.

This provision applies to VAT calculations under Polish VAT law for specific service sectors during designated transitional periods.
