Tax Calculation for VAT-Registered Service Providers in Trade and Catering – Poland

This provision establishes simplified VAT calculation methods for service providers in trade and catering, based on the applicable VAT rate applied to the delivered goods or services:

1. 18.03% – for goods and services taxed at 22%;
2. 6.54% – for goods and services taxed at 7%;
3. 4.76% – for goods and services taxed at 5%.

These rates are derived from the standard VAT rates, adjusted for the reverse charge mechanism or other applicable provisions under Polish VAT law.
