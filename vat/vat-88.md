**VAT Deduction & Refund Exclusions – Polish Tax Law (Art. 88–88.6)**

Exclusions from VAT deduction or refund adjustments apply to:  
- Hotel and catering services, except:  
  - Ready meals provided to passengers by transport service providers;  
  - Hotel services purchased for resale, taxed under Art. 8(2a).  
- Expenses referenced in Art. 29a(7)(3).  

Ineligible for deduction or refund adjustments:  
- Invoices issued by non-existent entities;  
- Transactions not subject to VAT or VAT-exempt;  
- Invoices or customs documents that:  
  - Report non-performed activities (partial exclusion);  
  - Contain inaccurate amounts (partial exclusion);  
  - Confirm activities governed by Art. 58 and 83 of the Civil Code (partial exclusion);  
- Buyer-issued invoices under separate rules not accepted by sellers;  
- Invoices lacking VAT amounts for taxable activities where VAT is stated (partial exclusion).  

Applies to duplicates and subsequent copies of invoices (Art. 3b).  

Also excluded:  
- Non-VAT registered active taxpayers (Art. 96), except as specified in Art. 86(2)(7).  

Excluded from deduction/refund:  
- VAT amounts under Art. 86(2)(4)(c), where intra-community acquisitions are taxed domestically due to incorrect VAT identification number (Art. 97(10)), if goods are located in another EU member state at time of delivery/transport completion.
