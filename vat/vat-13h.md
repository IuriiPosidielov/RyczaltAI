1. Movements of goods under the call-off stock warehouse procedure are not considered intra-community supplies under Article 13(3).

2. The call-off stock warehouse procedure applies if all of the following conditions are met:

   a) Goods are dispatched or transported by a VAT-registered EU taxpayer or a third party acting on their behalf, from the territory of the Member State to the territory of another Member State, for subsequent delivery and entry into a call-off stock warehouse, where the recipient (the VAT taxpayer) is entitled to treat the goods as owned by them, in accordance with a prior agreement between the parties;

   b) The sender or transporter of the goods does not have a business establishment or fixed place of business in the Member State to which the goods are being dispatched;

   c) The VAT taxpayer receiving the goods is identified for intra-community transactions within the destination Member State, and their name, legal identifier (for intra-community transactions), and identification number issued by that Member State are known to the sender at the time of dispatch or transport;

   d) The recipient taxpayer registers the movement of goods in the recordkeeping system under Article 109(11c) and includes the identification number from point (c) in the summary declaration under Article 100(1)(5).
