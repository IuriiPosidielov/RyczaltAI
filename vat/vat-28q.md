Public Finance Law — Administrative Procedure

The Minister responsible for public finance makes available, on the official website of the administrative unit serving the Minister, the notification form referred to in Article 28p, paragraph 1.

This provision establishes the obligation of the Minister to publish a standardized notification template for administrative purposes related to taxation, ensuring uniformity and transparency in public finance procedures.
