Tax Exemption for License and Sublicense Services – Transitional Provisions (Poland)

This provision exempts from tax the provision of licenses and sublicenses (excluding those specified in Article 4(2)(c) and (e) of the relevant law referenced in Article 175) granted under contracts entered into before the law’s effective date, regardless of any subsequent amendments to those contracts made after that date — provided such contracts were registered with the tax office before May 14, 2004. The exemption applies until December 31, 2005.
