Employment Law – Tax Documentation – Article 106c: Tax Invoices for Goods Delivered Under Article 18, Creating Tax Liability on the Debtor

Invoices documenting the delivery of goods referred to in Article 18, for which the debtor bears a tax obligation, shall be issued in the name and on behalf of the debtor by:

1) Executive bodies specified in the Act of June 17, 1966, on Enforcement Proceedings in Administration;
2) Court bailiffs performing enforcement actions within the scope of the Civil Procedure Code.
