Employment Law – Taxation of Vehicle Acquisition – Article 146ec

In 2023, for the purposes of Article 86(2)(7), the amount of tax due is the amount equivalent to 23% of the amount due for the delivery of a new means of transport, but not exceeding the amount of tax stated in the invoice or customs document confirming the acquisition of the means of transport, or the tax paid by the taxpayer for the intra-community acquisition of the means of transport.
