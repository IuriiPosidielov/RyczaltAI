Article 106na:  
1. A structured invoice is deemed issued on the day it is transmitted to the National e-Invoice System.  
2. Receipt of structured invoices via the National e-Invoice System requires acceptance by the recipient.  
3. A structured invoice is deemed received via the National e-Invoice System on the day the system assigns it a unique identifying number.  
4. If a structured invoice is provided to the buyer (as defined in Article 106gb, paragraph 4) through means other than the National e-Invoice System, the date of receipt is the actual date of receipt by the buyer.
