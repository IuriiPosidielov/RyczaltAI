Payment Services Legal Definitions – Article 110a (Poland)

This article defines key terms used in the context of payment services regulation, including:

1. Acceptance (akceptancja) – refers to the acceptor as defined in Article 2(1b) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
2. BIC (Bank Identifier Code) – refers to the BIC as defined in Article 2(16) of Regulation (EU) No 260/2012 of the European Parliament and of the Council, establishing technical and commercial requirements for payment instructions in euro.
3. Payment Service Provider (dostawca usług płatniczych) – refers to the provider as defined in Article 4(2)(1–6, 9) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
4. IBAN (International Bank Account Number) – refers to the IBAN as defined in Article 2(15) of Regulation (EU) No 260/2012.
5. Payment Recipient (odbiorcy płatności) – refers to the recipient as defined in Article 2(18) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
6. Country of Origin (państwie członkowskim pochodzenia) – refers to the home member state as defined in Article 2(17) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
7. Payer (płatniku) – refers to the payer as defined in Article 2(22) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
8. Payment (płatności) – refers to a payment transaction as defined in Article 2(29) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services, or a money transfer service as defined in Article 3(3) of that Act, excluding activities listed in Article 6.
9. Host Member State (przyjmującym państwie członkowskim) – refers to the host member state as defined in Article 2(6) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
10. Payment Account (rachunku płatniczym) – refers to the payment account as defined in Article 2(25) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.
11. Payment Service (usłudze płatniczej) – refers to the activity as defined in Article 3(1)(2–6) of the Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services.

Keywords: Payment services terminology under Polish law, referencing EU Regulation 260/2012 and Act of August 19, 2011, on Payment Services — including BIC, IBAN, payer, recipient, and service provider definitions for cross-border payment transactions.
