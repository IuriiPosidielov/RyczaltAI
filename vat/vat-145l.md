Polish Tax Law — Transitional Provisions on Electronic Invoicing (2026)

During the period from February 1, 2026, to March 31, 2026, taxpayers who, in 2024, had total sales (including VAT) not exceeding 200,000,000 zł, and who were required to issue structured invoices under prior regulations (Dz.U. 2023 poz. 1598, 2024 poz. 852, 2025 poz. 1203), may continue issuing invoices either electronically or in paper form.
