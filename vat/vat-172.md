Employment Law – Polish Civil Code Article 172  
This provision governs the classification of services performed under contracts governed by Article 7 ligne 1 point 2, where delivery occurred before May 1, 2004, and such activities were previously treated as services under the law referenced in Article 175 — maintaining that classification until completion of the activities.

Service classification under pre-2004 contractual delivery; continuity of service status until completion; governed by Article 7(1)(2) and Article 175 references.
