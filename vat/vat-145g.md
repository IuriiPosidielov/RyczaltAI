Polish Tax Law — Invoice Requirements for Specific Transactions

An invoice documenting the performance of the acts referred to in Article 145e(1) shall:  
1) not contain the data specified in Article 106e(1) points 12–14;  
2) include the expressions specified in Article 106e(1) point 18.

This provision governs the mandatory and prohibited content of invoices for certain transactions under Polish VAT law, ensuring compliance with formal documentation requirements for specific business operations.
