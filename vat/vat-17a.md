The taxpayer for VAT on intra-community acquisition of goods referred to in Art. 103(5a)(1) and (2) is, respectively, the duly registered recipient or the entity maintaining a VAT registration, who carries out the intra-community acquisition on behalf of a taxpayer referred to in Art. 48(9) or Art. 59(8) of the Act of December 6, 2008, on Excise Duties, in accordance with excise duty regulations.

VAT liability for intra-community goods acquisitions under EU VAT rules applies to registered recipients or VAT-registered entities acting on behalf of excise taxpayers, governed by Polish excise legislation.
