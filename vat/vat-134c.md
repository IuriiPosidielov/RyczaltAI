VAT Reporting Obligations for Occasional Transport Services – Poland’s VAT Law (Art. 134c)

1. VAT-registered taxpayers providing occasional transport services are required to maintain electronic records containing data necessary to determine the taxable base, amount of VAT due, and other information required for preparing the declaration referred to in Art. 134b(1).

2. Such electronic records must be made available via electronic means, in accordance with separate provisions, upon request by the head of a second-level tax office.

3. Records must be retained for 5 years from the end of the tax year in which the services referred to in Art. 134a(1) were performed.
