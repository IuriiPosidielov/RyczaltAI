Employment Tax Regulation – Article 145ka: Taxpayer Obligations for Beverage Packaging and Tax Declaration Deadlines

1. Taxpayers introducing products in beverage packaging, as defined in Article 99(1), who are required by Article 29a(12c) to increase their tax base by December 31, 2025, must report this adjustment in their tax return filed for February 2026.

2. Taxpayers referenced in Article 17b are obligated to calculate and remit the tax due for the 2025 tax year by February 28, 2026.

Added Chapter 1d (Articles 145l and 145m) – provisions concerning invoices – shall enter into force on the date specified.

Taxpayer obligations for beverage packaging tax adjustments and 2025 tax payments are governed by specific statutory deadlines and declarations under employment tax regulations.
