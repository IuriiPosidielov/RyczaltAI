Tax Law – Polish VAT Regulations – Article 112c (Additional Tax Liability for Incorrect Invoices)

In cases specified in Article 112b(1)(1), (2)(1), and (2a), where the error resulted from intentional actions by the taxpayer or their counterparty, of which the taxpayer was aware, and arises wholly or partially from a reduction in the tax due based on the tax calculated from an invoice that:

1) was issued by a non-existent entity;  
2) states activities that were not performed — to the extent of those activities;  
3) provides amounts inconsistent with reality — to the extent of those positions with inaccurate amounts;  
4) confirms activities subject to Articles 58 and 83 of the Civil Code — to the extent of those activities,

the additional tax liability for the portion of tax calculated based on such an invoice shall be 100%.

Paragraph 2: The provisions of paragraph 1 do not apply when determining additional tax liability in relation to individuals who, for the same act, are liable for a tax offense or criminal offense under tax law.
