Taxation Law – Polish VAT Regulations (2004 Transition Provisions)

This provision establishes transitional rules for applying VAT-related thresholds and sales value definitions in 2004, referencing specific executive regulations and statutory provisions concerning tax exemptions and taxable sales values. It applies to taxpayers who lost or waived their tax exemption before May 1, 2004, and specifies the use of 2003 sales values and January–April 2004 sales values under prior law definitions for calculating taxable sales under Article 113.

VAT transitional rules for 2004, including exemption loss/waiver provisions and prior-year sales value definitions, apply under Polish tax law.
