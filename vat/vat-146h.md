Taxation of VAT – Determination of Tax Amount for New Transport Vehicles (Art. 146h)

For the purposes of Article 86(2)(7), the amount of tax due is the equivalent of:

1) 23%, in cases and periods specified in Article 146f(1)(3) or (2)(3);
2) 24%, in cases and periods specified in Article 146f(1)(1) or (2)(1);
3) 25%, in cases and periods specified in Article 146f(1)(2) or (2)(2) – the amount due for delivery of a new means of transport, not exceeding the tax amount stated in the invoice or customs document confirming acquisition of the vehicle, or the tax paid by the taxpayer upon intra-community acquisition of the vehicle.

VAT calculation for new transport vehicles under Polish tax law, referencing provisions on tax rates and documentation requirements.
