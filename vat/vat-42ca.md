Legal Provision on Waiver of Art. 42c in Cases of Abuse of Law Proceedings

If the subject matter of the WIS (Wniosek Informacyjny Służby – Information Request to the Service) constitutes part of actions falling under a decision issued in connection with an abuse of law as referred to in Art. 5 para. 5, then Art. 42c shall not apply.

— This provision excludes the application of Art. 42c when the WIS relates to procedural actions tied to an abuse-of-law decision under Art. 5(5).
