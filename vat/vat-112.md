Tax Recordkeeping Obligations – Poland

Taxpayers are required to retain records prepared for tax settlement purposes and all related documents—particularly invoices—until the expiration of the tax liability statute of limitations, except as specified in Articles 109b §7, 130d §3, 134 §3, 138h §3, and 138i §8.

Taxpayers must maintain tax-related documentation and records for the duration of the statutory limitation period, with exceptions defined in specified provisions of the Polish tax code.
