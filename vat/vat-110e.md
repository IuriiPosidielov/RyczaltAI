Payment Services Law – Data Exchange Requirements

The record referred to in Article 110b(1) is made available by the payment service provider under the provisions of Article 24b(1) of Regulation (EU) No 904/2010 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 7 October 2010 on administrative cooperation and the fight against fraud in the field of value-added tax (Official Journal of the European Union L 268 of 12.10.2010, p. 1, as amended).

1) To the Member State of origin of the payment service provider; and in the case where the payment service provider provides payment services in a Member State other than its country of origin — to the receiving Member State.

2) To the Head of the National Tax Administration — in the case of payment service providers for which the Member State of origin or the receiving Member State is the Republic of Poland.

— Payment service provider data must be shared with relevant tax authorities in EU Member States, including Poland’s National Tax Administration, in accordance with EU VAT fraud prevention regulations.
