1. A farmer operating under a fixed-rate tax scheme who delivers agricultural products to a taxpayer liable for the agricultural tax is entitled to a fixed-rate tax refund concerning the purchase of certain agricultural production inputs subject to that tax. The refund amount is paid by the buyer of the agricultural products to the farmer.

2. The fixed-rate tax refund rate referred to in paragraph 1 amounts to 6.5% of the amount due for the delivery of agricultural products, reduced by the amount of the fixed-rate tax refund already granted.
