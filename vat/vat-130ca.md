VAT Declaration Correction Procedure – Poland (EU Member State)

This provision outlines the electronic submission process and content requirements for VAT declaration corrections in Poland, applicable when deadlines under Regulation (EU) No 282/2011 (Article 61(2) and Article 61a(1)) have expired. The taxpayer must submit corrections via the electronic application provided by the competent tax authority’s official website, specifically to the Łódź Tax Office.

The correction must include:
- Location and reasons for the correction;
- Taxpayer’s identifying data (name, address, VAT identification number, and country code);
- Tax period and VAT amount subject to correction;
- In case of overpayment, details necessary for settlement;
- Contact information (phone, email), including representative’s details if applicable.

Articles 130c(6) and (7) apply to the amounts subject to correction. All VAT amounts must be paid in euros to the Łódź Tax Office’s bank account.
