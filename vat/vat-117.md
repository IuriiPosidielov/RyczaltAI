Employment & Agricultural Tax Law – Polish Agricultural Levy Regulations

Art. 117. A farmer operating under a fixed-amount tax (ryczałt) in agricultural activities and delivering agricultural products is exempt from the obligation to:  
1) issue invoices;  
2) maintain records of goods and services supplied and received;  
3) submit tax returns to the tax office as provided in Art. 99(1);  
4) file registration declarations as provided in Art. 96.

Art. 118. The provisions of Art. 115, Art. 116(1–3a, 5–10), and Art. 117 shall apply in the case of agricultural service providers who are taxed under the fixed-amount system. These provisions shall apply to taxpayers who settle this tax.
