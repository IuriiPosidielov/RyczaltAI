Polish Tax Law – Temporary Tax Rate Provisions for Defense Budget Expenditures (2024–2025)

In the period from January 1, 2024, to the end of the year in which the total expenditures specified in Article 40(1)(2) of the Act of March 11, 2022, on National Defense (Journal of Laws 2024 No. 248, as amended) — as outlined in the budget law in force on July 31, 2024, and the financial plan of the Defense Support Fund submitted under Article 42(4) of the same Act, or the draft plan submitted to the Minister of National Defense under Articles 42(2) and (3) — exceed 3% of the gross domestic product (GDP) as defined in Article 40(2) of the Act, the following temporary tax rates apply:

1. Tax rate of 23% under Articles 41(1) and (13), 109(2), 110, 120(3a), and 138i(4);
2. Tax rate of 8% under Articles 41(2), 120(2) and (3), and Annex No. 3 to the Act;
3. Refundable tax credit rate of 7% under Article 115(2);
4. Tax withholding rate of 4% under Article 114(1).

The Minister responsible for public finance shall, via an official announcement in the “Monitor Polski” (Official Gazette of the Republic of Poland), declare the end of the period during which these rates apply, no later than October 31 of the year in which the conditions of Article 146(1) are satisfied.

Temporary defense-related tax rate provisions (2024–2025) in Polish tax law, triggered by defense spending exceeding 3% of GDP, with rates of 23%, 8%, 7%, and 4% — subject to formal termination by the finance minister by October 31 of the qualifying year.
