Employment and Commercial Transactions – VAT Accounting Obligations

Taxpayers making deliveries of goods or providing services listed in Annex 15 to the Act, as well as taxpayers acquiring such goods or services, are required to maintain an accounting record as defined in Article 49(1)(1) of the Act of August 29, 1997 – the Banking Act, or a named account in a cooperative savings and credit society opened in connection with their economic activity, denominated in Polish zloty.

Taxpayers engaged in VAT-liable transactions must maintain compliant accounting records under Polish VAT law, either via a bank account or a cooperative savings account, denominated in Polish zloty.
