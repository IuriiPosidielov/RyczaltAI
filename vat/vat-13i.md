If the conditions set forth in Article 13h(2) are met, an intracommunity supply of goods referred to in Article 13(1) shall be deemed to have taken place by the taxpayer referred to in Article 13h(2)(2), within the territory of the Member State at the moment ownership of the goods is transferred to the VAT taxpayer to whom the goods are to be delivered, provided that such transfer occurs within 12 months of the goods being introduced into the warehouse under the call-off stock procedure.
