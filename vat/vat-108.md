VAT Liability in Invoice Issuance — Polish Tax Law

1. Any legal entity, organizational unit without legal personality, or natural person issuing an invoice indicating the amount of VAT is liable for its payment.

2. Paragraph 1 applies accordingly if the taxpayer issues an invoice indicating a VAT amount higher than the VAT due.

3. In cases referred to in Art. 43(12a), public benefit organizations are liable for VAT payment.

4. (Repealed) Section 1a — Mechanism of Divided Payment

VAT liability arises upon invoice issuance by entities or individuals, including public benefit organizations in specified cases, under Polish VAT law.
