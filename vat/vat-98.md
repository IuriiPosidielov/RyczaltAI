**VAT Registration and Electronic Filing Regulations – Poland**

Article 98. Authority and Procedures for VAT Registration Forms and Electronic Submission

1. The Minister of Finance, by regulation, may:
   - Designate additional taxpayers (beyond those listed in Article 96(3)) who are not required to submit registration declarations;
   - Establish the formats for:
     - VAT registration declarations;
     - Certificates confirming VAT taxpayer registration;
     - Certificates confirming legal entity registration as a VAT taxpayer;
     - Certificates confirming VAT taxpayer status within the EU;
     - Declarations regarding cessation of taxable activities (as referenced in Article 96(1), (4), (6), and Article 97(9));
   - Define required elements of applications and confirmations under Article 97(17), and specify circumstances under which the designated authority shall issue written confirmations—ensuring accurate taxpayer identification and compliance with intra-EU transaction information exchange requirements.

2. The Minister of Finance, in coordination with the Minister of Informatization, may by regulation establish:
   - Procedures and technical organizational conditions for electronic submission and updating of:
     - VAT registration declarations;
     - Declarations regarding cessation of taxable activities (as referenced in Articles 96 and 97);
   - Structure of electronic submissions, while ensuring security, authenticity, non-repudiation, integrity, and time-stamping capabilities.
