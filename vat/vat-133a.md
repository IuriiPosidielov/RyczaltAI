VAT Declaration Correction – Foreign Entity Obligations under Polish Tax Law

In the event the deadline specified in Article 61(2) of Regulation 282/2011 expires, or in cases covered by Article 61a(1) of the same regulation, a foreign entity whose place of consumption is the Republic of Poland must submit a VAT declaration correction electronically via an information system provided by the competent ministry’s website and delivered to the Łódź Tax Office.

The correction must include:
1) Location and reasons for submission;
2) Foreign entity data: name, registered office address, VAT identification number issued by the member state and that state’s code;
3) Tax period and VAT amount subject to correction;
4) If overpayment occurred: details necessary for settlement;
5) Contact information (phone, email) of the foreign entity, or its representative if appointed.

Articles 133(5) and (6) apply to amounts subject to correction under paragraph 1.

The foreign entity must pay the corrected VAT amount in euros to the Łódź Tax Office’s bank account.
