Tax Representative Authority and Liability – Polish Tax Code

The tax representative acts on behalf of and for the taxpayer for whom they have been appointed:  
1) To fulfill the taxpayer’s obligations relating to tax settlement, including preparation of tax returns and summary reports as provided in Art. 100(1), as well as maintaining and preserving tax-related documentation and records;  
2) To perform other actions under the VAT and goods and services tax regulations, if specifically authorized under the agreement.

The tax representative is jointly liable with the taxpayer for any tax obligations settled on the taxpayer’s behalf.
