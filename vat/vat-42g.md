**Tax Administration — Issuance of WIS (Wniosek o Informację Skarbową)**

1. The WIS is issued without unnecessary delay, but no later than within 3 months of receipt of the application. This deadline excludes periods and intervals specified in art. 139 § 4 of the Tax Ordinance, as well as days during which the e-Tax Office experienced technical failure, as provided in art. 35e ust. 4a of the Act of November 16, 2016, on the National Tax Administration.

2. For matters concerning WIS, provisions of Part IV of the Tax Ordinance apply, excluding Chapters 17, 18, 19, and 20.

3. The Director of the National Tax Information Office may issue a decision denying the WIS if the application concerns goods or services referred to in art. 42b ust. 5 pkt 2, but such goods or services do not constitute a single taxable activity.

4. All correspondence regarding WIS must be submitted and delivered exclusively via the e-Tax Office account, except as otherwise provided in art. 35e ust. 9 of the Act of November 16, 2016, on the National Tax Administration.

5. Physical samples of goods and documents that cannot be submitted through the e-Tax Office or whose physical delivery is necessary for processing the WIS application — including accurate classification of goods, services, or combinations thereof constituting a single taxable activity — are submitted under general rules.

— This text outlines procedural rules for issuing WIS within tax administration, including deadlines, applicable legal provisions, submission methods, and exceptions for physical documentation.
