In the period referred to in Article 146ef, for the purposes of Article 86(2)(7), the amount of tax due is the amount equivalent to 23% of the amount due for the delivery of a new means of transport, but not exceeding the amount of tax indicated in the invoice or customs document confirming the acquisition of that means of transport, or the tax paid by the taxpayer for the intra-community acquisition of that means of transport.

This provision relates to VAT adjustment mechanisms for new vehicle acquisitions during transitional periods, governed by Polish VAT law.
