Tax Exemption for Animal Feed and Fertilizers Imported via Transport Vehicles Used for Animal Transit

Article 76 provides for exemption from import tax on animal feed, fertilizers, and feedstuffs imported via transport vehicles used to convey animals from third-country territory into the domestic territory, intended for use by the animals during transit.

Import tax exemption applies to animal feed, fertilizers, and feedstuffs transported via vehicles used for animal transit from third countries into domestic territory, intended for use by animals during transport.
