Payment Service Provider Transaction Record Requirements – EU Payment Services Directive (PSD2) Compliance

This article establishes the mandatory elements to be included in the payment transaction record (as defined in Art. 110b para. 1), required for cross-border payments and refund transactions. The record must contain: (1) a unique identifier for the payment service provider (e.g., BIC); (2) the payee’s name or legal name as held by the provider; (3) a tax or VAT identification number or equivalent if available; (4) IBAN or alternative identifier with location data if IBAN is unavailable; (5) BIC or identifier of the provider acting on behalf of the payee if the payee lacks a payment account; (6) the payee’s address if available; and (7) for cross-border payments or identified refunds: (a) payment/refund date and time; (b) amount and currency; (c) country of origin or destination (as per Art. 110b para. 3); (d) additional uniquely identifying information; and (e) proof of physical presence at point of sale if initiated in a retail/service point.
