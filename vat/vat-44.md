Exemption from intra-community VAT on acquisitions:  
1) Goods subject to the provisions of Art. 43(1)(5)–(8);  
2) Goods whose import under specified import conditions would be exempt from VAT.

Intra-community VAT exemption applies to acquisitions of goods covered under Art. 43(1)(5)–(8) or goods imported under conditions granting VAT exemption.

VAT exemption for intra-community acquisitions; applies to goods covered by Art. 43(1)(5)–(8) or goods imported under VAT-exempt conditions.
