Binding tax rate information (WIS) — legal definition for VAT and customs purposes

This article defines “Binding Tax Rate Information” (WIS) as an official decision issued to determine the applicable tax rate for:  
- Delivery of goods,  
- Import of goods,  
- Intra-community acquisition of goods,  
- Provision of services.  

The WIS must include:  
1) Description of the goods or services subject to the WIS;  
2) Classification of the goods or services using:  
   - Harmonized System (HS) codes (Chapter, Heading, Subheading, or CN code),  
   - Polish Classification of Products and Services (Chapter, Group, Class, Category, Subcategory, or Position),  
   - Polish Classification of Construction Objects (Section, Chapter, Group, or Class),  
   - Services classified under Polish Classification of Products and Services (Chapter, Group, Class, Category, Subcategory, or Position),  
   — to enable:  
      a) Determination of the correct tax rate,  
      b) Application of tax law and implementing regulations — where applicable under Art. 42b(4);  
3) The applicable tax rate, unless otherwise specified under Art. 42b(4).
