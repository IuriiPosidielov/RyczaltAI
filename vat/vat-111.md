Tax Law – VAT Registration & Fiscal Receipt Systems (Poland)

This text outlines the legal obligations for taxpayers in Poland who sell goods or services to individuals or agricultural producers under the simplified tax regime, requiring the use of fiscal cash registers (kas rejestrujących) for recording sales. It details mandatory documentation, technical standards for cash registers, penalties for non-compliance, refund and deduction rules for register purchases, and administrative procedures for register certification and maintenance. It also addresses exceptions, special cases (e.g., natural disasters), and responsibilities of manufacturers, service providers, and tax authorities. The provisions are governed by the Polish VAT Act and enforced by the National Tax Administration.

Polish VAT law requires fiscal cash register use for sales to private individuals and agricultural producers, mandates documentation of sales data, enforces technical standards, and imposes penalties for non-compliance
