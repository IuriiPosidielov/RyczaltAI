VAT Group Liability and Intracommunity Transactions — Solidarity Obligations of VAT Group Members

Art. 8e. During the period in which a VAT group holds the status of a taxpayer, and also after the group loses that status, its members jointly and severally bear responsibility for the group’s VAT liabilities.

Chapter 3: Intracommunity Acquisition of Goods and Intracommunity Supply of Goods

VAT group members remain jointly liable for the group’s tax obligations both during and after the group’s VAT taxpayer status, including obligations arising from intracommunity transactions. This provision governs the continuity of liability and applies to both intra-EU acquisitions and deliveries of goods within the EU framework.
