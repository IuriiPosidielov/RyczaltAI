The Minister of Finance, by means of regulation, during the period referred to in Article 146ef, may reduce tax rates to 0%, 5%, or 8% for the supply of certain goods and provision of certain services, or for parts of such supplies or services, and may establish the conditions for applying the reduced rates. When issuing such a regulation, the Minister shall take into account: (1) the specific nature of trade in certain goods and provision of certain services; (2) the execution of the state budget.

Tax rate reductions (0%, 5%, 8%) for specific goods/services under Polish tax law, subject to ministerial regulation and budgetary considerations.
