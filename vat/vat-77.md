**Tax Exemption for Fuel and Lubricants Carried in Transport Vehicles – Polish Tax Code Article 77**

This provision establishes tax exemptions for fuel and lubricants carried in transport vehicles under specific conditions, including:

- Fuel transported in standard tanks (up to 200 liters per vehicle) for private or commercial transport vehicles, and in portable containers (up to 10 liters) for private vehicles intended for on-board use.
- Lubricants necessary for vehicle operation, stored within the transport vehicle.
- Definitions of “standard tank,” “commercial transport vehicle,” “special container,” and “private transport vehicle” are specified to ensure compliance.
- Exempt goods must be used exclusively within the transport vehicle in which they were imported and may not be removed, stored, or transferred — except for necessary repairs.
- Violations result in tax liability calculated based on the goods’ declared value and applicable rates at the time of violation.
