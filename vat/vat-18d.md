VAT Representative Obligations for Non-Registered Importers – EU Intracommunity Trade

In the case of a taxpayer who: (1) carries out exclusively imports of goods destined for the territory of another EU Member State and exports those goods from the territory of the country through the importer acting within the framework of intra-EU goods delivery, and (2) is not registered as an active VAT taxpayer — the VAT representative performs, in their own name and on behalf of the taxpayer, the obligations of the taxpayer as set forth in Art. 18c(1)(1), and — if the contract so provides — other activities of the taxpayer as specified in Art. 18c(1)(2).

The taxpayer referred to in paragraph 1 jointly and severally liable with the VAT representative for any tax liability that the representative settles on behalf of the taxpayer.
