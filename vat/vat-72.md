Tax Exemption for Import of Design and Patent Documentation for Intellectual Property Authorities

Art. 72 provides for tax exemption on the import of signs, patterns, sketches, designs, and accompanying documentation and patent applications intended for institutions responsible for the protection of copyrights or patents.

This provision applies to intellectual property-related materials imported for official use by designated national or international IP authorities.

Tax exemption applies specifically to import duties on design and patent documentation destined for copyright or patent protection institutions.
