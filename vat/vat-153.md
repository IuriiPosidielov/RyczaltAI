Employment Law – Polish Taxation of Co-Ownership Contributions

1. For the purposes of taxation as specified in Article 29, paragraphs 6 and 7, the values of contributions and fees referred to in this article, as well as portions of such contributions and fees paid prior to May 1, 2004, are excluded from taxable base.

2. The provisions of the Act do not apply in cases referred to in Article 29, paragraph 8, if the establishment of a co-ownership right to residential premises and actual occupation of such premises occurred before May 1, 2004.

— Taxation of co-ownership contributions in Polish housing cooperatives, with retroactive exclusions prior to May 1, 2004, under employment-related housing law provisions.
