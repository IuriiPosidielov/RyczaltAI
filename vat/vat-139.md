Electronic and Paper-Based Registers of Accounting Records – Special Provisions

Articles 139–145 (omitted) — Chapter 1a: Special provisions concerning accounting registers with electronic or paper-based copies

This section addresses regulatory provisions related to the maintenance and management of accounting records in both electronic and paper formats, focusing on procedural and compliance aspects of record-keeping under applicable financial or tax law frameworks.
