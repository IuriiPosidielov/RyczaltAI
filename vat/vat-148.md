Polish Tax Law – VAT Sales Tax Calculation Rules

For the purposes of calculating the taxable sales amount as referred to in Article 7, paragraphs 4–6, for the year 2003 and for the period from January 1 to April 30, 2004, the value of taxable goods sales shall be determined in accordance with the interpretation set forth in Article 4, point 8, of the law referenced in Article 175.
