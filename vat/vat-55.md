Art. 55. Exemption from import duty, on the basis of reciprocity, applies to seeds, fertilizers, and agricultural products intended for use in farms located within the country, operated by individuals managing primary farms situated on the territory of a third country bordering the country, provided the following conditions are jointly met: (1) the quantity of imported products does not exceed the amount necessary for the cultivation or operation of the farm; (2) customs declaration of the products is made by the farmer or on their behalf.

Exemption from import duties for agricultural goods is granted under mutual reciprocity terms for farmers operating in bordering third countries, subject to quantity limits and customs declaration by the farmer.
