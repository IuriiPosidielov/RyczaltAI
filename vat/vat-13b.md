Customs Law — Intracommunity Acquisition of Goods — Legal Recognition of Place of Acquisition

If the conditions set forth in Article 13a, paragraph 2, are met, the acquisition of goods referred to in Article 9, paragraph 1, within the European Union shall be deemed to have taken place on the territory of the Member State of the taxpayer referred to in Article 13a, paragraph 2, point 3, at the moment the taxpayer acquires ownership rights over the goods, provided that such transfer occurs within 12 months of the goods being introduced into the warehouse under the call-off stock procedure.
