VAT Calculation Rules for Services in Trade and Catering – Poland (Art. 146g)

This provision establishes the VAT calculation method for services—including those in trade and catering—under specific conditions outlined in Art. 146f, for the purposes of Art. 85. It defines reduced VAT rates applied as a percentage of the delivery value, depending on the underlying VAT rate applicable to the goods or services.

- For cases under Art. 146f(1)(1) or (2)(1):  
  - 19.36% for goods/services taxed at 24%  
  - 8.26% for goods/services taxed at 9%  
  - 5.66% for goods/services taxed at 6%

- For cases under Art. 146f(1)(2) or (2)(2):  
  - 20% for goods/services taxed at 25%  
  - 9.09% for goods/services taxed at 10%

- For cases under Art. 146f(1)(3) or (2)(3):  
  - 18.70% for goods/services taxed at 23%  
  - 7.41% for goods/services taxed at 8%

VAT calculation for services in trade and catering is governed by reduced rates under Art. 146g, depending on the underlying VAT rate and specific conditions from Art. 146f, for application under Art. 85.
