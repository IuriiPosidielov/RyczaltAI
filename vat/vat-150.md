In the case provided for in Article 21d of the law referred to in Article 175, receipt of a document confirming export of goods outside the country after April 30, 2004, entitles the taxpayer to adjust the VAT due on that sale, no later than within six months from the end of the month in which the sale was made, as defined in Article 4(8) of the law referred to in Article 175.

VAT adjustment permitted post-export documentation receipt, within 6-month statutory window from sale month end, under specified statutory conditions.
