Import Tax Exemption for Scientific and Educational Use — Article 57

1. Import is exempt from tax for:  
   (1) Animals specially prepared for laboratory use;  
   (2) Non-domestically produced biological or chemical substances suitable primarily for scientific purposes, imported in quantities not indicating commercial intent.

2. Exemptions under paragraph 1 apply only if goods are destined for:  
   (1) Public institutions whose primary activity is education or conducting scientific research, or for designated units within such institutions, provided those units’ primary activity is scientific research or education;  
   (2) Private institutions whose primary activity is education or conducting scientific research.
