VAT Law – Payment Mechanism for Tax Withheld by Taxpayer (Art. 103a)

In cases where tax is collected by the taxpayer referred to in Art. 17a, the taxpayer referred to in Art. 103 para. 5a may remit the tax amount to the VAT payer’s account using the payment message specified in Art. 108a para. 3. The taxpayer must include the following information in the message:  
1) In points 1 and 2 — the tax amount specified in Art. 103 para. 5a, intended for payment under the split-payment mechanism;  
2) In point 3 — the document number issued by the taxpayer under Art. 17a related to the payment;  
3) In point 4 — the identifier used to recognize the taxpayer for tax purposes.
