Employment Law – Polish Labor Code Provision: Calculation and Carryover of Annual Allowance Amounts

Article 151 establishes the amounts for the period May–December 2004 as 30,500 zł and 107,000 zł, proportionally adjusted from provisions in Articles 10(1)(2) and 24(2), and mandates these same amounts to be applied for 2005 under Articles 10(2) and 24(3) as the applicable annual limits.

Employment law provision defining proportional adjustment and carryover of allowance amounts for 2004–2005 under Polish labor regulations.
