Polish Tax Law – VAT Exemption for Specific Agricultural and Food Products (2022)

During the period from February 1, 2022, to December 31, 2022, the VAT rate was zero percent for:

1. Foodstuffs listed in positions 1–18 of Annex 10 to the Act, excluding those classified under PKWiU 56 (services related to food supply);
2. Soil improvers, growth stimulators, and substrates for cultivation, as defined in the Act of July 10, 2007, on fertilizers and fertilization (Journal of Laws 2021 No. 76, 2022 No. 1370 and 2364), excluding mineral substrates;
3. Fertilizers and plant protection products, typically intended for agricultural production, listed in position 10 of Annex 3 to the Act;
4. Garden soil listed in position 12 of Annex 3 to the Act;
5. Natural gas (CN codes 2711 11 00 or 2711 21 00).

Paragraphs 1 points 1–4 do not apply to imports of goods referred to in Article 138i, paragraph 1.

Summary: VAT exemption provisions for agricultural inputs, food products, and natural gas under Polish tax law during 2022; excludes imports covered by Article 138i.
