Polish Tax Law – VAT Calculation for Services in 2023

In the case of taxable persons providing services, including in trade and gastronomy, the tax due for purposes of Article 85 is calculated as the product of the delivery value and the applicable rate:  
1) 18.70% – for goods and services taxed at 23%;  
2) 7.41% – for goods and services taxed at 8%.
