Art. 66. Exemption from tax on imports, on the basis of mutual reciprocity, applies to:  
1) Gifts offered to heads of state and government officials, and persons entitled to the same privileges under international agreements;  
2) Goods intended for use or consumption by foreign heads of state and government officials during their official stay on the territory of the country.
