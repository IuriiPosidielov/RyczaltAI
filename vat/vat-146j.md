Art. 146j. – VAT rules for artworks delivered under 8%, 9%, or 10% tax rates (Art. 120(11)(3))

The provisions of Article 120(11)(3) apply accordingly to the delivery of artworks that were subject to VAT at the 8%, 9%, or 10% rate applicable to such goods under Article 146f.
