Taxation – Definitions and Scope of Taxpayer Status (Article 28a)

For the purposes of this Chapter:

1) The term “taxpayer” refers to:
   a) Entities engaged in independent economic activity as defined in Article 15(2), or economic activity equivalent to such activity, regardless of its purpose or outcome, in accordance with Article 15(6);
   b) Legal persons not qualifying as taxpayers under paragraph a), but identified or required to be identified for tax or VAT purposes.

2) A taxpayer who also carries out activities or enters into transactions not considered taxable supplies of goods or services under Article 5(1) is deemed a taxpayer for all services rendered on its behalf.

Taxpayer definition under Polish tax law, including self-employed individuals, legal persons identified for VAT purposes, and scope of taxpayer status for non-taxable transactions.
