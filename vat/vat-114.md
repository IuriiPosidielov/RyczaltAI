Taxation of Personal Taxi Services – Ryczałt Provision for Taxpayers

Taxpayers providing personal taxi services, excluding the rental of passenger cars with driver (PKWiU 49.32.11.0), may opt for a 3% flat-rate taxation of such services, provided they notify the head of the tax office in writing by the end of the month preceding the tax period in which the flat rate will be applied. When applying this flat-rate taxation, Article 86 does not apply. Taxpayers opting for the flat rate must submit a shortened tax return within the timeframe specified in Article 99(1), with Articles 99(7a) and (7b) applying accordingly. A taxpayer may revoke the flat-rate choice no earlier than after 12 months, provided they notify the head of the tax office in writing by the end of the month preceding the month from which they will no longer apply the flat rate. The Minister of Finance shall, by regulation, define the form and instructions for the shortened tax return referred to in paragraph 3, including submission deadlines and locations. Paragraph 6 (revoked) pertains to special procedures for flat-rate farmers.

Taxation of personal taxi services under flat-rate regime — taxpayer notification, 3% rate, exemption from Article 86, shortened return filing, revocation after 12 months, ministerial regulation on form and submission.
