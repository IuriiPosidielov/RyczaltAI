Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Under Article 2(27g), regional or local magazines refer to periodical publications (excluding CN 4902 and CN 4911) focused on regional or local topics, also intended for blind and visually impaired individuals. These publications are issued no more than once a week, in separate issues (numbers) under a common title, with no predetermined end date. They are generally published at regular intervals and printed in editions of no more than 15,000 copies.
