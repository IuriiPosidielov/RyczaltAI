Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. In Article 2(1), the term "aircraft" refers to aircraft weighing 1550 kilograms or more, if used for no more than 40 working days or from the moment they were put into service, no more than three months, except for air transport referred to in Article 83, paragraph 1, point 6. At the time of admission, the aircraft is considered to be put into service on the day it was put into service, as indicated by the manufacturer to the first purchaser, or the date on which it was first used for demonstration purposes by the manufacturer.
