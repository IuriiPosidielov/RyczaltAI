Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Under Article 2(25a), telecommunications services refer to services involving the transmission, emission, and reception of signals, texts, images, sounds, or any kind of information through cable, radio, optical, or other electromagnetic systems. This also includes the transfer or assignment of rights to use the means for providing such transmission, emission, or reception, as well as providing access to global information networks, in accordance with Article 6a of Council Implementing Regulation (EU) No 282/2011 of 15 March.
