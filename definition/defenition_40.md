Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. In Article 2(27ba), "actual value" shall be understood as the actual value referred to in Article 1(48) of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2015/2446 of 28 July 2015, supplementing Regulation (EU) No 952/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council as regards detailed rules relating to certain provisions of the Union Customs Code (OJ EU L 343 of 29.12.2015, p. 1, as amended), hereinafter referred to as "Regulation 2015/2446".
