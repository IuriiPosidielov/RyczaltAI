Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Specifically, Article 2(41) defines “bonie” as an instrument connected with the obligation to accept it as consideration or part of consideration for the delivery of goods or the provision of services, where the goods to be supplied, the services to be provided, or the identity of potential suppliers or service providers are indicated in the instrument or set out in related documentation, including the terms and conditions for the use of this instrument.
