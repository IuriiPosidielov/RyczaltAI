Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Under Article 2(21), agricultural services include: a) services related to agriculture and animal breeding, excluding veterinary services (PKWiU ex 01.6), horse shoeing services, and running animal shelters on farms (PKWiU ex 01.62.10.0); b) services related to forestry (PKWiU ex 02.40.10), excluding forest patrolling carried out on behalf of entities other than forestry services (PKWiU ex 02.40.10.3); c) services supporting fishing (PKWiU ex 03.00.7), excluding services related to sea fishing (PKWiU 03.00.71.0); d) rental and leasing of agricultural machinery and equipment without operator (PKWiU 77.31.10.0).
