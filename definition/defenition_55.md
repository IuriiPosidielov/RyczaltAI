Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. In Article 2(37), the term "VAT account" shall be understood as the VAT account referred to in: a) Article 62a, section 1 of the Act of 29 August 1997 on Banking Law (Journal of Laws U. of 2024, items 1646, 1685 and 1863, and of 2025, items 146, 222 and 525); b) Article 3b, section 1 of the Act of 5 November 2009 on cooperative credit unions savings and credit (Journal of Laws of 2025, item 379).
