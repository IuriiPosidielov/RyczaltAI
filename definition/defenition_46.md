Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Under Article 2(30), “ex” refers to a scope of goods and services narrower than the one specified in a given chapter, heading, subheading, or nomenclature code, as appropriate, within the combined classification (CN) or a given grouping of classifications issued based on regulations on public statistics. Article 2(31) defines “invoice” as a document, either in paper form or in electronic form, containing data required by law and regulations issued on its basis. Article 2(32) defines “electronic invoice” as an invoice in electronic form. The provisions of Article 2, points 31 and 32, will come into force on February 1, 2026, as published in the Journal of Laws of 2023.
