Article 2 of the Polish VAT Act defines key terms used in the Act. Under Article 2(15), agricultural activity includes plant and animal production, such as the production of seeds, nursery and breeding material, and reproductive, vegetable, field, greenhouse, and underground crop production. It also covers ornamental plants, cultivated mushrooms, fruit growing, breeding and production of breeding material for animals, birds, and insects, utility animals, industrial or farm animal production, breeding and raising fish and other organisms living in water, as well as crops grown in greenhouses and heated foil tunnels, mushroom cultivation and mycelium production, in vitro plant cultivation, farm breeding and rearing of slaughter poultry and laying hens, poultry hatcheries, breeding and rearing of fur animals, breeding of earthworms, entomophages, and silkworms, running apiaries, and breeding other animals outside agricultural holdings, as well as the sale of forestry products.
